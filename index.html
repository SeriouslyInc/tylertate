Design, code, coffee. I enjoy creating products people love.

I'm cofounder and CEO of Crema.co, the coffee marketplace. Before that I was cofounder at Twigkit (enterprise search software), the first design lead at Nutshell (SaaS CRM), and coauthor of the book Designing the Search Experience.

Originally from Alabama, I lived in the United Kingdom (London and Cambridge) for seven years, and currently reside in Seattle, Washington.
