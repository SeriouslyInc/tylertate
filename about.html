About

I believe that deep customer empathy combined with a data-driven mindset are the prerequisites of a winning strategy.

I believe that tightly scoped, thoughtfully executed products that are tested experimentally and then iterated on are the most impactful.

I believe that always treating others with respect, cultivating ownership, and helping others succeed are the ingredients of a thriving team.

I believe that a growth mindset, scrappiness, and undeterred grit are the pathway to achieve personal growth.
